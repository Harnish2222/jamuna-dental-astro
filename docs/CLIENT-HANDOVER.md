## 1. CMS Guide (How to Edit Your Website)

Your website is powered by **TinaCMS**, which allows you to edit content directly on the page visually.

### How to Access the Editor:
1. Go to your live website URL and add `/admin` to the end.
   - Example: `https://your-domain.com/admin`
2. Log in using the credentials provided below.
3. You will see your website exactly as it appears to customers, but with an editing sidebar on the left.

### How to Edit Content:
- **Text & Headlines**: Click on any text box in the left sidebar to change headlines, paragraphs, and buttons. You will see the changes update in real-time on the right side of your screen.
- **Images**: To change an image (like in the "Clinic Gallery" or "Price Teller" section), click the image field in the sidebar. This will open the Media Manager where you can upload new photos or select existing ones.
- **Testimonials & Services**: To add a new review or treatment, find the respective list in the sidebar, click "Add Item", and fill in the text and stars.
- **Saving**: Once you are happy with your changes, click the **Save** button in the top left corner. The live website will update automatically within 1-2 minutes.
