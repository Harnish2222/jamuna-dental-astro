### Orthodontic Excellence in Dindigul

A straight smile is more than just about looks — it's about better oral health and function. At **Jamuna Family Dental Care**, we provide comprehensive **orthodontic treatment in Dindigul** for children, teens, and adults.

#### Why Choose Braces?

Orthodontic treatment can correct various dental issues, including:
- Crooked or crowded teeth
- Gaps between teeth
- Misaligned bites (overbite, underbite, crossbite)

#### Our Braces Options

*   **Metal Braces**: The most traditional and cost-effective way to straighten teeth, now smaller and more comfortable than ever.
*   **Ceramic Braces**: These use clear or tooth-colored brackets, making them less noticeable than metal braces.
*   **Self-Ligating Braces**: These use a specialized clip instead of elastics, which can lead to faster treatment times and fewer adjustments.
